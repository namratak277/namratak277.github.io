🧶 Crochet

Crochet is my creative outlet and a way to disconnect from the digital world. I love the meditative process of working with yarn and hook to create beautiful, functional pieces. From blankets and scarves to amigurumi figures, each project brings a sense of accomplishment and relaxation. It's a wonderful blend of patience, creativity, and tangible results.

🎨 Watercolor Painting

Watercolor painting allows me to express myself through color and composition. I enjoy experimenting with techniques, creating landscapes, florals, and abstract pieces. The fluidity of watercolor mirrors the dynamic nature of creativity—you plan, but you also adapt and let the medium guide you. It's a beautiful way to capture emotions and moments on paper.
